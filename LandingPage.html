Our Mission

Five years ago, a group of friends came together when they realized that they didn't want to throw away their new books, but needed to make space for other things. They decided to give new life to those old favorites, repairing the covers and cleaning their marks before putting them up for sale.

The project grew and eventually a physical shop was established where people came to donate their own books and purchase "new" old ones from us

Thus, the Dr. Sharp bookstore came to be.
